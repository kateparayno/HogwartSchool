TRANSFIGURATION

Transfiguration is the art of changing the form or appearance of an object, and hence
this is what this class teaches. Transfiguration is a theory-based subject,
including topics such as "Switching Spells" (altering only a part of some object, such
as giving a human rabbit's ears); Vanishing Spells (causing an object to completely

disappear); and Conjuring Spells (creating objects out of thin air). It is possible
