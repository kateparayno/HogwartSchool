History of Magic


History of Magic is the study of magical history. Cuthbert Binns' lessons are depicted
as some of the most boring at Hogwarts. They are only lectures, given without pause,
about significant events in wizarding history. Topics have included goblin rebellions,
giant wars, and the origins of wizarding secrecy. This is the only class at Hogwarts
that is taught by a ghost, as the professor never noticed he had died and simply
continued teaching as if nothing had changed. The only known teacher of this subject is
Professor Cuthbert Binns (17th or 18th century-?).
