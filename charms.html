Charms

Charms the type of spell concerned with giving an object new and unexpected properties,
and hence this class mainly consists on learning those sorts of spells. Charms
classes are described as notoriously noisy and chaotic, as the lessons are largely
practical. Many of the exposition sequences in the books are set in Charms classes,
which are on the second floor of Hogwarts. The class is taught by Professor Flitwick
(c. 1976-?, possibly before).
