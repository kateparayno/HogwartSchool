Apparition

Apparition is the magical form of teleportation in the wizarding world. Lessons are
optional to those in the sixth and seventh years. In the wizarding world, performing
Apparition requires a licence and may only be legally performed by people over seventeen
years of age. The described reason for the restriction is that Apparition is
dangerous if done improperly: insufficient concentration may lead to body parts being
left behind in an unfortunate side-effect known as splinching. Although, as Hermione
points out innumerable times throughout the series, magical enchantments on Hogwarts
castle and grounds prevent Apparition and Disapparition inside the castle, these
protections are temporarily relaxed within the Great Hall for short periods to permit
students to practise Apparition. Wilkie Twycross, a Ministry of Magic Apparition
Instructor, offers lessons in Apparition to sixth-years.
