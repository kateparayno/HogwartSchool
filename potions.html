Potions


Potions is described as the art of creating mixtures with magical effects. It requires
the correct mixing and stirring of ingredients at the right times and temperatures.
Snape's lessons are depicted as unhappy, oppressing times set in a gloomy dungeon in
the basement of the castle, whilst Slughorn's, who replaces Snape as Potions Master,
is shown as more cheerful and even fun at times. The teachers of this class have been

Professor Horace Slughorn (?-1981) (1996-?) and Professor Severus Snape (1981-1996).
